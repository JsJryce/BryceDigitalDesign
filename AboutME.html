Who I Am & What I Am Passionate About

My name is Bryce Robinson, and I am deeply passionate about technology, gaming, and creativity. I enjoy building computers, playing basketball, and exploring virtual worlds through video games. These interests fuel my drive to create and innovate.

What I Do & My Niche

I specialize in creation and design, with a focus on digital tools and platforms. I work primarily with Blender for 3D modeling, Unreal Engine 5 for game development, and have some experience with HTML for web design. My passion lies in bringing ideas to life through technology and creativity.

Why I Do It

My motivation stems from a lifelong love of video games and a strong desire to express my creativity. This passion inspired me to pursue this program as my summer job, where I can combine my interests with hands-on experience. I am driven to constantly improve my skills and contribute to projects that challenge and excite me.

What Makes Me Unique

What sets me apart is my prior experience with these platforms, which allows me to approach them from a unique perspective. I am able to think more creatively and apply what I’ve learned in new ways, enhancing my ability to problem-solve and innovate.

What I’m Looking For

I am currently seeking internship opportunities that will allow me to further develop my skills, deepen my knowledge, and grow professionally in the field of digital creation. I am eager to contribute to meaningful projects and expand my understanding of the industry.
